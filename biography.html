Biography

<–

Shachaf Pereg (1994) is an artist based in London and Amsterdam. She is a master's student in MA Contemporary Art Practice from the Royal College of Art, London, received her BA in Fine Arts from the Gerrit Rietveld Academie, Amsterdam, and wrote her Bachler Thesis Education to Norms. She received her photography diploma from the Gavra Photography Institution, Tel Aviv.





Recent exhibitions include Gerrit Rietveld Academie, Amsterdam (2024), m4 gastatelier, Amsterdam (2024), The Rietveld Pavilion, Amsterdam (2024), De Hallen, Amsterdam (2023), and Gavra Photography Institution, Tel Aviv (2019). She has been granted the Cultuurfondsbeurs of the Cultuurfonds (2024), Merit Scholarships, California Institute of the Arts (2024), the Profile Fund of the Gerrit Rietveld Academie (2021–2024), and the Holland Scholarship of the Dutch Ministry of Education, Culture and Science (2020). She worked and volunteered as a tutor with a wide diversity of students with different abilities and backgrounds.
